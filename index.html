WORDLE SOLVER

A Wordle solver is a tool designed to help players solve the popular word-guessing game, Wordle. It takes the player's previous guesses and feedback (correct letters, wrong letters, and letters in the wrong position) to narrow down possible words. By analyzing patterns and eliminating incorrect possibilities, the solver suggests the most probable words to guess next.
